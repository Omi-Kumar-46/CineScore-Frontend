    Frequently Asked Questions

    Everything you need to know about our prediction engine.

    How accurate are the predictions?

    Our neural network operates at an 87% historical accuracy rate for domestic opening weekends. The model is trained on over two decades of box office data, continuously refining its weights based on real-time market shifts.

    What metrics do you analyze?

    We ingest thousands of data points including presale velocity, historical franchise decay, actor bankability, social sentiment velocity, and macro-economic factors.

    Do I need to connect my studio's API?

    No. CineScore automatically aggregates public data across the internet. However, direct studio API integration is available for enterprise clients looking to track proprietary pre-release screening data.

    How frequently is data updated?

    The hype-meter and sentiment trackers update in real-time. Core box office projections run major algorithmic optimization sweeps daily at 00:00 UTC.

    Can I use this for indie or limited releases?

    Currently, the V1.0 architecture is highly optimized for wide theatrical releases (2,000+ screens). We are actively training a specialized neural net for limited and indie distribution models.

    How do I access the Director's Hub?

    The Director's Hub is a private dashboard requiring an authenticated account. You can create a free Beta account using the Sign Up button to start actively tracking up to 6 releases.
