The Origin Story

Where Cinematic Passion
meets Predictive Precision

CineScore isn't a corporate product. It is a passion project engineered to bridge the scattered market of global cinema with the absolute precision of data science. We harness the power of machine learning to decode patterns in audience behavior, franchise performance, and cultural trends to provide unparalleled foresight into the future of Cinema

The CineScore Ethos

Our Identity

Who We Are

Welcome to CineScore, where we merge the art of cinema with the rigid science of analytics. Our team comprises industry-obsessed developers and data architects who possess a profound understanding of the entertainment sector. We pioneer quantitative analysis for the modern theatrical landscape.

Pursuit of Excellence

What We Do

We exist to eliminate the guesswork from the film industry. By aggregating vast datasets—from social sentiment velocities to historical franchise decay rates—we translate the unpredictable art of storytelling into calculated financial projections. We empower creators with Wall Street-level analytics.

Mission Statement

The Commitment

Transparency over hype. Architecture over aesthetics. We are committed to building an elite, enterprise-grade machine learning platform that scales without relying on bloated libraries. CineScore provides actionable intelligence that empowers our users to make informed decisions in an ever-evolving market.

Omi Kumar

Founder & Head of Studio • Lead Developer • ML Engineer

I bridge the gap between two deeply scattered worlds: the boundless magic of global cinema and the ruthless logic of data science. I deeply believe in the power of storytelling and meaningful experiences. On the other hand, I am also an unabashed nerd, a die-hard cinephile, and a melophile obsessed with curating a perfect, hand-picked 1,500-track playlist.
